About the Hackathon

Join us for Hack2Intern 2024, where developers, designers, and innovators come together to solve real-world problems! The hackathon will provide a platform for participants to collaborate, learn, and showcase their creativity.

Event Schedule

  • Opening Ceremony: 9 AM, November 29, 2024
  • Hackathon Start: 10 AM, November 29, 2024
  • Submission Deadline: 12 PM, November 30, 2024
  • Closing Ceremony: 2 PM, November 30, 2024

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams must consist of 2-4 members.
  • Participants must submit their code by the deadline.
  • Use only open-source technologies for development.
  • Judging will be based on creativity, functionality, and presentation.
